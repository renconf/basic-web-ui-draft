Talking about my wallpaper collections

Brief Summary

- Collecting wallpapers as a hobby taps into our natural urge to acquire and appreciate various items. Here are some reasons why I collect wallpapers:

  1. Uniqueness and Pleasure: The rarity of a wallpaper piece can make it valuable and stimulate parts of the brain associated with pleasure. Collectors seek the unique, which excites their brains and provides intellectual satisfaction.
  2. Pride and Recognition: Acquiring exquisite wallpapers and gathering like items together can set collectors apart from their peers. Finding a rare piece brings joy and pride, especially when it garners recognition and admiration from others.
  3. Sense of History: Owning antique wallpapers allows collectors to feel closer to the past, important people, or long-gone circumstances. Some hope to build a legacy by passing these special items to future generations.
  4. Stress Relief: Hobbies like collecting wallpapers can help relieve stress, providing a positive outlet for creativity and exploration.
